Dublin

Ireland’s bustling capital city has something for everyone – amazing museums and historical sites, beautiful parks, plenty of charming pubs and traditional music venues. And, of course, the Guinness Storehouse. The highlight of our time in the city was a bike tour. Our trusty guide, Brian, took us on a fascinating ride that covered just about everything, from the city’s beginnings as a Viking settlement to the port of Dublin, where Facebook and Google have their Irish headquarters. (With a stop for a pint in between, of course!)
